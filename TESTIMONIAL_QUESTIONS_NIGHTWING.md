## üìä Follow-Up Questions (If They Give Short Answers)

**If they say "It's great!" ask:**

- "What specifically makes it great?"
- "What problem did it solve for you?"
- "How has it changed your daily operations?"

**If they mention metrics, ask:**

- "Can you share specific numbers?" (orders/day, time saved, etc.)
- "How does that compare to before?"

**If they're hesitant about permission:**

- "We can keep it completely anonymous if you prefer"
- "We'll show you the final case study before publishing"
- "You can review and approve the quote before we use it"

## üí° Tips for Getting Great Testimonials

1. **Make it easy:** Send via WhatsApp if that's how you normally communicate
2. **Be specific:** Ask about concrete benefits (time saved, errors eliminated)
3. **Follow up:** If they give a short answer, ask one more specific question
4. **Show value:** Explain how their testimonial helps other restaurants
5. **Respect privacy:** Always offer anonymous option
6. **Timing:** Ask when they're happy with the system (not during problems)
